Nefarious Noodles

by Nancy

Cook Time: 20 minutes




Ingredients:

- 1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced

- 2 cups egg noodles, cooked

- 1 cup broccoli, cut into florets

- 1/2 cup carrots, julienned

- 1/2 cup snap peas, sliced

- 1 cup baby spinach leaves

- 1/4 cup soy sauce

- 2 cloves garlic, minced

- 1 tablespoon sesame oil

- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce

- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet, heat sesame oil over medium heat.

2. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.

3. Add thinly sliced chicken and cook until browned.

4. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and pepper.

5. Add broccoli, carrots, snap peas, and cook for 5 minutes.

6. Toss in baby spinach and cook until wilted.

7. Mix in cooked egg noodles until well-coated and heated through.

8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Nefarious Noodles with a total cook time of 20 minutes!
