Brazen Burger

by Jade

Cook Time: 30 minutes




Brazen Burger:

- 1 lb ground beef

- 4 hamburger buns

- 4 slices of bacon, cooked

- 4 slices of cheddar cheese

- 1 cup lettuce, shredded

- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced

- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced

- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise

- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

- Salt and pepper to taste

- 2 tablespoons olive oil (for cooking)

Instructions:

1. Preheat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat.

2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Season with salt and pepper.

3. Drizzle olive oil in the hot skillet or on the grill. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes per side or until they reach your preferred level of doneness.

4. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and cover to melt.

5. While the burgers are cooking, toast the hamburger buns in the skillet or on the grill until golden.

6. Assemble the burgers by placing a cooked patty on the bottom half of each bun.

7. Spread mayonnaise and Dijon mustard on the top half of the buns.

8. Top the burgers with bacon slices, shredded lettuce, sliced cherry tomatoes, and red onion.

9. Cover with the top half of the bun.

10. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Brazen Burger with a total cook time of 30 minutes!

Feel free to customize the recipe to your liking. Add your favorite condiments and toppings for a personalized touch. Enjoy your flavorful Brazen Burger!
