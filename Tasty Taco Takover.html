Tasty Taco Takeover

by Jade

Cook Time: 25 minutes




Ingredients:

- 1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced

- 1 white onion, finely chopped

- 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped

- 1 tablespoon olive oil

- Salt and pepper to taste

- Taco shells or tortillas

- Optional toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, avocado slices, lime wedges

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.

2. Add thinly sliced chicken breast to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

3. Add chopped white onion to the skillet and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.

4. Season chicken and onions with salt and pepper to taste.

5. Warm up taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.

6. Assemble tacos by placing a spoonful of chicken and onion mixture onto each taco shell or tortilla.

7. Top with chopped cilantro and any desired toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, avocado slices, and a squeeze of lime juice.

8. Serve immediately and enjoy your Tasty Taco Takeover!

Feel free to customize the recipe to your liking. Enjoy your delicious Tasty Taco Takeover with a total cook time of 25 minutes!
