Crazy Corn

by Jose

Cook Time: 20 minutes




Ingredients:

- 4 ears of corn, husked

- 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)

- 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

- 1 teaspoon chili powder

- 1/2 teaspoon cumin

- Salt and pepper to taste

- 2 tablespoons butter, melted

- Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

2. In a small bowl, mix together the grated cheese, chopped cilantro, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to create a spice blend.

3. Brush each ear of corn with melted butter, making sure to coat evenly.

4. Sprinkle the spice blend over the buttered corn, ensuring even coverage.

5. Place the corn on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the corn is tender and has a nice char.

6. Remove the corn from the grill and squeeze lime wedges over each ear.

7. Serve the Crazy Corn hot and enjoy the burst of flavors!

Feel free to customize the recipe by adding more spices or adjusting the cheese and cilantro quantities according to your taste preferences. Enjoy your quick and delicious Crazy Corn with a total cook time of 20 minutes!
