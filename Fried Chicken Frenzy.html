Fried Chicken Frenzy

by Fredrick

Cook Time: 30 minutes




Ingredients:

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

- 1 cup buttermilk

- 1 cup all-purpose flour

- 1 teaspoon garlic powder

- 1 teaspoon onion powder

- 1 teaspoon paprika

- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust for spice preference)

- Salt and black pepper to taste

- Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, combine buttermilk, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.

2. Place chicken breasts in the buttermilk mixture, ensuring they are well-coated. Marinate for at least 10 minutes.

3. In a separate bowl, mix flour, additional salt, and black pepper to taste.

4. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.

5. Dredge each marinated chicken breast in the flour mixture, making sure they are evenly coated.

6. Carefully place the coated chicken breasts in the hot oil and fry for about 5-6 minutes per side or until golden brown and cooked through.

7. Remove the fried chicken from the skillet and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil.

8. Serve the Fried Chicken Frenzy with your favorite dipping sauce or sides.

Feel free to customize the recipe to your liking. Enjoy your delicious Fried Chicken Frenzy with a total cook time of 30 minutes!
